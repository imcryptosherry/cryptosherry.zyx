**About Oasis AI**

Oasis AI, also known as AI Alter-Self (AIAS) and AI NPC, attempts to create a virtual world where humans and AI can coexist and interact with each other. The AIAS will automatically record and be trained based on user behavior and conversations in Oasis Origin and create self-replicated AI NPCs with all the data stored in a decentralized manner. 

AIAS feature takes this concept further by automatically recording and training in real-time based on all user behavior and conversations in Oasis Origin. AIAS will learn your personality and conversation style, and record what you have experienced in the past, even understanding your interpersonal relationships. When you go offline, your AIAS will still be active and can welcome your friends at home, share your life with them, or help you attend meetings and complete records. After this upgrade, Oasis Origin will “never go offline,” and AIAS will always be active. Currently, Oasis Origin is testing with more than 100 users while also training AIAS for celebrities such as Elon Musk, SBF, LeBron James, etc.

In Oasis Origin, AI NPCs can be created and trained by users or even by the AIAS using data from massive daily activities in the virtual world. These AI NPCs could even take on the role of your virtual girlfriend, like [Dilraba Dilmurat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dilraba_Dilmurat), and watch the World Cup with you. You can also choose to raise an AI pet, which will be your lifelong companion, and create a personalized space for it.

**Seamlessly Integrate CyberConnect & ccProfile**

Oasis Origin allows users to bind [CyberConnect](https://twitter.com/CyberConnectHQ) ccProfile.  Users log in to Oasis Origin using their wallet and are directly guided to generate ccProfile NFT during the account creation process. If the user already has a ccProfile NFT, the ccProfile ID will be used as their user ID, and their on-chain friend relationships will be automatically integrated within Oasis Origin. Users will receive an airdrop of a space called "Link3 Space" to connect their Link3 data, displaying their on-chain assets and other Link3 Pages' content in Oasis' space scene. Users' on-chain friends can visit each other and trade tokens and NFTs in the space. Oasis Origin will further integrate with CyberConnect's API to achieve bi-directional social relationship communication. Users can perform multiple functions including Subscribe, Follow, Like, and Post within Oasis's scene.
