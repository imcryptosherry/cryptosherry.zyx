What is background of Oasis Crush?

Oasis Crush is an AI virtual persona NFT launched by Oasis, a leading player in the metaverse industry. Since its establishment in 2018, Oasis has built a metaverse social product that attracted 20 million Gen-Z users in the Web2 era, with an average daily usage time of over 110 minutes. Within just two months of its launch, Oasis ranked first on the social app charts in Brazil and gained prominent positions in countries like the United States and the Philippines. Due to its rapid growth and impressive performance, Oasis has successfully secured $10 million in Series B funding, led by investors such as 5Y Capital, BAI Capital and Oasis Capital.This funding reinforces Oasis' position and potential in shaping the future of the metaverse industry.

Oasis Crush：An AI Personality NFT

Oasis AI tackles the creation of AI personalities, a trillion-dollar market with vast commercial potential. Whether as a digital avatar, virtual friend, partner, or even romantic companion, AI offers valuable opportunities.

Introducing Oasis Crush, an AI personality NFT that seamlessly integrates with metaverse applications. Using LLM technology and customized AI models, users can tailor their AI persona's learning and mimicry, shaping a unique virtual personality. With customizable appearance, voice, and skills, Oasis Crush opens up lucrative avenues in fan economy, virtual romance, and emotional well-being.

The underlying AI NPC models leverage LLM and are supported by the Knowledge Graph Database, storing personalized information. Early on, this data was centralized, but plans are underway to migrate it to Greenfield, an on-chain storage protocol built on BNBChain. This migration aims to achieve "on-chain AI personalities" and enables AI contracts to execute specific actions across all Web3 environments.

Moreover, the new Oasis version introduces MPC solutions, ensuring a seamless transition for millions of existing users into the Web3 world. By eliminating the need for complex processes like mnemonic phrase memorization, Oasis emerges as a professional SuperApp bridging Web2 and Web3 realms.
