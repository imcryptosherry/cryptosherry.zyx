Oasis AI x CyberConnect | Building for Metaverse Coexistence

April 4th, 2023

Author: CryptoSherry

Oasis has 10M registered users in 130+ countries, and nearly 1M daily active users (source: paoka), spending 110 min/day on activities like sports, live streams, concerts, and board games. Accessible on mobile, VR, and web. Recently, Oasis announced a major Web3 upgrade and launched Oasis Origin on BSC. Oasis Origin, a decentralized virtual world, integrates GPT-4 and is introducing an innovative feature called the “Oasis AI”.

About Oasis AI

Oasis AI, also known as AI Alter-Self (AIAS) and AI NPC, attempts to create a virtual world where humans and AI can coexist and interact with each other. The AIAS will automatically record and be trained based on user behavior and conversations in Oasis Origin and create self-replicated AI NPCs with all the data stored in a decentralized manner.

AIAS feature takes this concept further by automatically recording and training in real-time based on all user behavior and conversations in Oasis Origin. AIAS will learn your personality and conversation style, and record what you have experienced in the past, even understanding your interpersonal relationships. When you go offline, your AIAS will still be active and can welcome your friends at home, share your life with them, or help you attend meetings and complete records. After this upgrade, Oasis Origin will “never go offline,” and AIAS will always be active. Currently, Oasis Origin is testing with more than 100 users while also training AIAS for celebrities such as Elon Musk, SBF, LeBron James, etc.

In Oasis Origin, AI NPCs can be created and trained by users or even by the AIAS using data from massive daily activities in the virtual world. These AI NPCs could even take on the role of your virtual girlfriend, like Dilraba Dilmurat, and watch the World Cup with you. You can also choose to raise an AI pet, which will be your lifelong companion, and create a personalized space for it.

Tech Highlights

  • GPT4 helps multi-model training faster and better in Oasis Origin.
  • Multi-platform supports Web, iOS, Android, PC, and VR. Several patents of VR through the years.
  • Pluggable AI model, which is on-chain and can be used in other Dapps.
  • Massively multiplayer metaverse, with Web3 native functions such as a stake, mint, swap, or trade.

Team

Oasis AI team has talents in different areas. Members have years of experience in AI development, global growth, and Web3 technics. The core members of the original Oasis are all in this new project, along with AI experts who have worked in Google and ChatGPT.

Seamlessly Integrate CyberConnect & ccProfile

Oasis Origin allows users to bind CyberConnect ccProfile. Users log in to Oasis Origin using their wallet and are directly guided to generate ccProfile NFT during the account creation process. If the user already has a ccProfile NFT, the ccProfile ID will be used as their user ID, and their on-chain friend relationships will be automatically integrated within Oasis Origin. Users will receive an airdrop of a space called "Link3 Space" to connect their Link3 data, displaying their on-chain assets and other Link3 Pages' content in Oasis' space scene. Users' on-chain friends can visit each other and trade tokens and NFTs in the space. Oasis Origin will further integrate with CyberConnect's API to achieve bi-directional social relationship communication. Users can perform multiple functions including Subscribe, Follow, Like, and Post within Oasis's scene.

Connected 2023 Hackathon and Upcoming Collaborations

Oasis Origin has secured the coveted 1st Prize in the BNB CHAIN & CyberConnect Connected2023 Web3 Social Hackathon, as announced on their official Twitter page. As a result of integrating CyberConnect, Oasis Origin is planning a series of collaborations, including an exclusive Community Call in Cyber Square, a virtual event space that provides a large-scale interactive environment with selectable unique avatars for hosts and capacity for thousands of attendees. In Cyber Square, the audience can engage with the hosts and other guests, share comments via bullet screens, and even participate in video chats when permitted. Once the audience is invited to the podium or applies to speak, they will be automatically transported to the stage and given the option to select their host avatar. This event promises to be a unique and exciting experience for all participants.
