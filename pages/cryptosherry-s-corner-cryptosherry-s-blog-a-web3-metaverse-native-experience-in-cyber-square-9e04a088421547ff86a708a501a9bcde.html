A Web3 Metaverse Native Experience in Cyber Square

April 22, 2023

Author: CryptoSherry

On April 7th, CyberConnect held its inaugural web3 metaverse native community call at Cyber Square, a customized space on Oasis Origin.

Community members submitted their wallet addresses through a community call announcement on Discord to gain access to Cyber Square on Oasis Origin. Upon entering, users appear as standardized "Slim" avatars, allowing them to freely interact with hosts and other users through bullet screens. In the future, users will also have the option to display their own NFTs when entering the square. The hosts on the main stage were Pisofa.eth and kevinlili.eth from the Cyber Community, who displayed CyberConnect's official website to users as their unique customized avatars.

The audience could apply to speak and was automatically transported to the main stage when given permission to do so. Additionally, users' wallets automatically received Cyber Connect's airdrop upon entering the space. The scene peaked with over 500 online users, and those who were unable to enter the space could still listen in on the Discord community call channel. These immersive social experiences are unique compared to traditional Twitter Spaces and Discord channels.

The current tested version supports up to a thousand users on the same screen, and the space will be expanded in the future to accommodate up to ten thousand users simultaneously. The next version of Oasis Origin Square is currently in beta testing and will feature AI NFT minting, adding a more entertaining element to users' web3 social experiences. The AI NFT functionality will be released in the next iteration, so stay tuned for more updates!
